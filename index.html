BREAKING THE
ECHO CHAMBER

Algorithmic Interventions for Eating Disorder Stigma: Shifting the Burden from User Willpower to Platform Architecture

RESEARCH OBJECTIVES

  • > Goal: Investigate effective algorithmic interventions to reduce ED stigma.
  • > Shift Burden: Move safety responsibility from adolescent willpower to platform architecture.
  • > Provide Framework: Concrete guide for policymakers & engineering teams.

METHODOLOGY

Qualitative Content Analysis

Analysis of primary digital artifacts and tech policy documents through the lens of Humane Technology.

DATA COLLECTION TikTok UI mechanics, Transparency Reports, Community Guidelines.
ANALYSIS LENS Center for Humane Technology "Ethical Design" Principles.

THEORETICAL FRAMEWORK

Social Comparison Theory (Festinger)

TikTok hyper-accelerates "Upward Comparisons" (idealized bodies) leading to dissatisfaction.

Stigma Model (Griffiths)

Algorithm implicitly "values" disordered bodies with high engagement, reinforcing self-stigma.

LIMITATIONS

  1. Access: No access to source code; limited to public UI & Policy docs.
  2. Time: Constraints prevented longitudinal study of intervention effects.

THE "VALIDATION LOOP"

How the architecture traps the user

1. Insecurity

User seeks validation for body issues.

2. Engagement

Algo logs "Interest" in harmful content.

3. Cultivation

"Echo Chamber" validates self-stigma.

THE EVIDENCE: "THEY KNEW"

Meta (Project Mercury)

Internal research proved Instagram worsened body image for 1 in 3 teen girls but prioritized engagement.

TikTok Time Limits

Execs knew prompts only reduced usage by 1.5 minutes, yet launched them as primary "safety features".

WHY CURRENT FIXES FAIL

1. ALGO-SPEAK

Bans fail because users evolve language.

"anorexia" → "anoreksja"

2. CO-OCCURRENCE

Safe tags like #recovery are cross-pollinated with toxic content by the AI.

THE FRICTION MISMATCH

Harmful Content ZERO FRICTION

Auto-play, Infinite Scroll

Help Resources HIGH FRICTION

Search, Click, Read

ARCHITECTURAL SOLUTIONS

1. Algorithmic Palate Cleansers

If "high-risk cluster" detected, system forcibly injects neutral content (Nature, Comedy) to break the flow state.

2. Zero Metric Mode

A safety toggle hiding Likes/Views. Stops ED from becoming a competitive sport of numbers.

3. Professional Context

Verified experts add "Context Notes" to viral trends, fighting misinformation with trusted authority.

"Adolescents cannot fight a supercomputer with willpower alone. If the trap is algorithmic, the cure must be algorithmic."
